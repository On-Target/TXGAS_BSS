Andy Burton

IT Manager

Andy is the IT Manager for Centric Infrastructure Group. He is responsible for the build out and continued development of an innovative, robust, and secure information technology environment throughout the company.

During his time at Centric, Andy initiated Centric’s first cybersecurity training program and is currently in the process of bringing endpoint management and endpoint protection back in-house as well as setting up a mobile device management program.

Before Joining Centric, Andy worked as an IT consultant at ExxonMobil. Prior to that, he worked as an IT consultant at Houston Methodist Hospital, The Woodlands, where he assisted in the design and creation of the IT equipment layout plan during the construction of the facility. Andy brings over 25 years of IT experience to Centric Infrastructure Group.

Outside of work, Andy enjoys sports, RV-ing, cooking and spending time with his wife, two sons and two dogs.
