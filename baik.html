John Baik


VP of Strategic Finance

Mr. John Baik serves as Vice President, Finance at Centric. Where he focuses on the diligence, structuring, and financing of all the company’s strategic growth opportunities. John has over five years of private equity and investment banking experience.

Prior to joining Centric Infrastructure, John served as Senior Associate at Ara Partners in Houston, TX, where he executed and monitored over $250 million of direct investments from Fund I and II. Prior to Ara Partners, John executed over $1 billion in M&A transactions and public market offerings at Oppenheimer & Co’s Investment Banking Division.

John holds a BS in Commerce from the University of Virginia.

John lives in Houston, TX, and enjoys traveling and hiking with his wife and dog.
