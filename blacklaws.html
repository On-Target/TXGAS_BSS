Andreya Blacklaws

Project Coordinator

Over the past two years, Mrs. Andrea Blacklaws has held multiple roles of increasing responsibility at Centric Infrastructure Group. Currently, she serves as Project Coordinator for the Gas Department. As Project Coordinator, she is responsible for project reporting, the implementation of a new project management software, and the oversight of project coordination & field operations logistics.

Prior to joining Centric Infrastructure, Mrs. Blacklaws worked as the Director of Golf Logistics at Spectrum Events. During her seven-year tenure, she was responsible for setting up the entire catering/concession operation for various PGA golf tournaments. She also held an important role, as the Customer Service / Finance Manager, in the Music Festival Division. She was responsible for the implementation of the Point-Of-Sale system used to capture concession sales. As well, accounting for all transactions, recaps, and settlement payments related to the sales of the event.

Mrs. Blacklaws received her degree from The Art Institute of Houston. She hopes to further her education by attending online classes towards a Business Management degree.

Outside of work, Mrs. Blacklaws enjoys spending time outdoors with her husband and daughter. She enjoys creative activities such as baking/decorating, interior design, and party planning.
