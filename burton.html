Chris Burton

Vice President,
Fiber Installation

Mr. Burton serves as Vice President, Fiber Installation at Centric where he is responsible for the company’s daily Fiber operations as well as its construction activities. In addition, Christopher oversees and administers EHS processes to ensure the company’s employees work in a safe and responsible manner.

Mr. Burton brings 26 years of experience in the telecommunications industry and has led teams across the United States and in several international markets. During his career, Christopher has accomplished building data center networks, long-haul infrastructure, and fiber optic services to residential (single and multi-family)/commercial projects.

In his spare time, Christopher enjoys spending quality time with his family, being a father, outdoor activities, and his personal walk with God.
