Forest Caveney

Corporate Controller

Forest Caveney serves as Corporate Controller of Centric, responsible for Accounting, Reporting, and Controls. Working for the CFO, Forest manages a top-notch team of analysts, accountants, and specialists to ensure accurate financial reporting and robust business controls.

Prior to joining Centric, Forest worked for Exxon Mobil Corporation for 13 years, holding various financial positions in Reporting, Controls, Audit, and Business Analysis. During this time, he lived in Louisiana, Texas, Abu Dhabi, and the UAE, where he was the Country Controller for almost four years. Prior to his time at Exxon, Forest was a Director at Sigma Financial Corporation in Ann Arbor, MI for eight years where he was responsible for Accounting, Commissions, and Human Resources.

Forest holds a bachelor’s degree in Finance from Mount Saint Mary’s University and a Master of Business Administration from the University of Michigan.

Outside of work, Forest enjoys running, live music, movies, traveling, and spending time with his wife and two children.
