Roswell “Ros” Dixon

Director,
IT & Network Operations

Mr. Roswell “Ros” Dixon serves as the Director of Information Technology for Centric where he leads the growth and transformation of Information Technology. He directs and coordinates the development, implementation, and deployment of all technology solutions.

Prior to joining Centric, Mr. Dixon worked as the Deputy CIO Infrastructure for the City of Houston and Director of IT for Boardwalk Pipelines Partners. He began his career serving 25 years in the United States Marine Corps. During his career he commanded at the Platoon, Company and Battalion levels. He has several combat tours serving in various leadership positions.

Mr. Dixon holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Prairie View A&M University and a Master of Information Technology Management.

Outside of work, Mr. Dixon enjoys spending time with his wife, traveling, playing golf, fishing and hunting.

Centric has grown by bringing clean, safe, affordable gas to Texas consumers for 27 years. As we accelerate our growth with new infrastructure offerings, we will continue to grow with the values we hold dear, always keeping the needs of our customers as the highest priority.”

Robert S. Barnwell, IV
