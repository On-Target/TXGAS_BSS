Amy Doyal

Director,
Regulatory Compliance

Ms. Doyal serves as the Director, Regulatory Compliance. During her 15+ years at the company, she has been a central contributor in developing the regulatory department and acting as the liaison to The Railroad Commission of Texas (RRC). Amy has held several roles at Centric, ranging from Administration, Business Development, Billing, Regulatory and IT.

Currently, Ms. Doyal is responsible for managing and participating in all matters related to regulatory compliance, preparing reports, and analyses for the RRC as well as analyzing and reporting of the cost of natural gas. She is also tasked with scheduling and supervising the financial and safety audits with the RRC.

Amy received her Bachelor of Science in Education from the University of Nebraska where she played softball for the nationally ranked Huskers. She holds a Master's in Education Administration from Arizona State University where she was also the Assistant Softball Coach for the Division 1 program.

Outside of work, Amy enjoys spending time with her husband, children, grandkids, dogs, playing golf, hiking, and kayaking.
