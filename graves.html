Billy Graves

Vice President, Construction

Mr. Graves serves as the Vice President, Construction for Centric and has been with the company since 2013. He has played an active role in the company’s growth into emerging markets and he has been instrumental in the development and construction of the natural gas infrastructure to acquire new market share.

Billy is responsible for the day-to-day operations and is heavily involved with the company’s strategic planning. His field knowledge and operations experience has been pivotal in the expansion of the Company.

Mr. Graves has worked in the natural gas field for over 17 years. Previously, he worked for Mississippi River Gas in Port Gibson, Mississippi. He started as a field technician and shortly after he was promoted to an Operations Manager in charge of operations for the State of Texas.

Mr. Graves attended Texas State Technical College in Waco, Texas and received a degree in Machinist Engineering Technology. 

Outside of work, Mr. Graves enjoys fishing, traveling, being active outside, and spending time with his wife and two children.
