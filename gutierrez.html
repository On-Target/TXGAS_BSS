Nayely Gutierrez

Engineer

Nayely serves as an Engineer for Texas Gas Utility Services. During her time at Texas Gas, she has helped design natural gas and fiber optic systems to serve projects throughout all Texas markets. She works with Executive Management and the engineering team to ensure natural gas systems are robust, reliable, safe, and delivered in a timely manner.

Nayely is primarily responsible for designing efficient and safe natural gas systems. She also serves as the liaison between Texas Gas and different governmental agencies to receive permit approvals at a city, county, and state-level. Nayely also works with the field operations team to ensure a seamless construction process for timely delivery of services.

Prior to joining Texas Gas, Nayely worked in Los Angeles, California as a Project Engineer for local construction company, a national general contractor. She brings with her over 2 years of successful engineering, construction operations, and problem-solving experience.

Nayely holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of Southern California. After living in California for 5 years, she is excited to be back in her hometown of Houston, TX. Outside of work, Nayely enjoys traveling, camping, playing softball, walking her dog, Peanut, and spending time with friends and family.
