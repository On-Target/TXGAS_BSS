Carol Hawkins

Executive Assistant & Office Administrator

Ms. Hawkins serves as the Executive Assistant and Office Administrator for Centric Infrastructure Group. Carol is new to the team, but not new to the position. She brings more than 20 years of support experience and expertise, primarily to the C-Suite and senior management levels. She is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations and administrative support needs of the office. She will be a key part of growth and logistical coordination, business development efforts, as well as providing critical support to the Executive Management Team and Board of Directors. Carol is diversified, talented, and brings years of experience in event planning management.

Prior to joining Centric Infrastructure, Ms. Hawkins worked as the Sr. Executive Assistant, supporting the Global Vice President of Human Resources with a major oilfield services corporation in Houston.

Ms. Hawkins attended Trevecca Nazarene University and is currently enrolled with the ACPWC, working towards her certification as a Planning and Event Consultant, a skillset that will prove to enhance her role and contribution to the company.

Outside of work, she is a strong supporter of community events, church involvement and charities and specifically an advocate of the Inspiration Ranch Charity, which works with special needs children with the aid of therapeutic horses. Ms. Hawkins has three children and six grandchildren and spends time with them as often as possible. Ms. Hawkins also enjoys time with close friends, repurposing antique furniture, sailing and cycling.
