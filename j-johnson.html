Joey Johnson

Vice President,
Supply Chain

Mr. Johnson serves as the Vice President, Supply Chain for Centric. He works closely with Company leadership to ensure infrastructure is efficiently and accurately tracked, sufficient stocks of inventory, and timely delivery of materials for all projects. Joey is ultimately responsible for overseeing all GIS, Inventory, and Purchasing activity at Centric.

Before joining Centric, Joey worked as the OSP Fiber GIS and Drafting Manager for a leading fiber optic and telecommunications Company based in Houston. Prior to his time there, he worked for a pipeline engineering firm in the Houston area serving as the OSP GIS & Drafting Manager for a national fiber optic company based in Dallas and Houston. Joey also worked as the CADTEL GIS & Drafting Manager for a Kansas based COOP phone company.

Joey brings a total of 43 years of GIS, Mapping & Drafting experience to the Centric Fiber team! Joey holds a Drafting Certification from Shreveport Vo-Tech. Outside of work, he enjoys spending time with his family and friends, 4 wheeling and camping, hunting, scuba diving and cooking.
