Joey Johnson

Vice President,
Supply Chain

Joey serves as the GIS and Inventory Manager for Centric Fiber. He works closely with Company leadership and the engineering team for fiber optic purchasing and inventory management for all projects across Texas.

Additionally, Joey is responsible for the OSP Fiber GIS system and management of all data returned from the field to assure proper documentation of all fiber assets and accurate tracking of fiber splicing in the field.

Before joining Centric Fiber, Joey worked as the OSP Fiber GIS and Drafting Manager for a leading fiber optic and telecommunications Company based in Houston. Prior to his time there, he worked for a pipeline engineering firm in the Houston area serving as the OSP GIS & Drafting Manager for a national fiber optic company based in Dallas and Houston. Joey also worked as the CADTEL GIS & Drafting Manager for a Kansas based COOP phone company.

Joey brings a total of 42 years of GIS, Mapping & Drafting experience to the Centric Fiber team! Joey holds a Drafting Certification from Shreveport Vo-Tech. Outside of work, he enjoys spending time with his family and friends, 4 wheeling and camping, hunting, scuba diving and cooking.
