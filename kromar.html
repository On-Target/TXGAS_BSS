Tammie Kromar

Director, Accounting & Risk Management

Tammie Kromar serves as the Director, Accounting & Risk Management for Centric where she is responsible for handling financial and accounting services, risk management, and she serves as a key liaison for annual audit preparation, cash management and interactions with the Executive Team.

Tammie brings over 20 years of accounting experience to Centric, with 14 of those years spent in the utility services industry. She is a valuable asset to the company leading our team of accounting, accounts payable, and accounts receivable personnel. Tammie is leading the implementation of new technologies to streamline our processes to better serve our customers as we continue to acquire significant market share in the growth markets pursued by our Executive and Business Development teams.

Prior to joining Centric, Ms. Kromar worked as the Controller for HMW Special Utility District, a political subdivision of the State of Texas, providing water and sewer services in the Magnolia area. 

Tammie holds a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy from the University of Phoenix. Outside of work, she enjoys traveling and spending time with family and her three grandchildren.
