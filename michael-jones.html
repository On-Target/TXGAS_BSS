Michael Jones

Vice President and Corporate Controller

Michael Jones serves as Vice President and Corporate Controller at Centric, overseeing the company's Accounting function since 2024. An accomplished finance leader with over 20 years of experience, Michael has excelled in both private and public organizations.

Specializing in financial reporting, treasury management, and strategic planning, he is known for driving financial performance and operational excellence. Michael has successfully led transformative initiatives, including ERP implementations, acquisition integrations, and cash flow optimization, consistently delivering measurable results.

As graduate of Southern Illinois University, Michael earned a Bachelor of Science in Accounting. Outside of the office, Michael enjoys spending time with his wife and four children.
