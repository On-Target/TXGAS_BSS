Beth Morgan

Business Development Manager

Beth serves as Manager of Business Development for Centric Infrastructure Group. She is responsible for identifying, developing, and executing on growth opportunities with existing and new partners.

Beth is local to the Dallas–Fort Worth area and works alongside the rest of the Business Development Team to ensure the Company continues to expand the provision of safe, reliable, and economical end-user services to rapidly growing markets across the State of Texas, with a focus on the greater Dallas-Ft. Worth region.

Prior to joining Centric Infrastructure Group, Beth spent eight years in Houston working in the financial services industry. She served as Corporate Banker for a publicly traded San Antonio-based bank, where she managed a multimillion-dollar portfolio through new and existing client growth. Following her six-year tenure as a Commercial and Industrial Lender, she spent two years as a fully licensed Wealth Manager for a national firm. Since that time, Beth moved back to her hometown of Dallas where she and her husband currently reside.

Beth holds a Bachelor of Business Administration in Investment Banking and Corporate Finance from The University of Texas at Austin. Outside of work, Beth enjoys traveling, cooking, exercising, and spending time outdoors with her husband and dog.
