Jason Palmenberg

Chief Technology Officer

Mr. Palmenberg serves as the Chief Technology Officer of Centric Infrastructure Group where he is responsible for technical operations, innovation, and overall growth of the company. He has over 19 years of experience in the energy and infrastructure industry.

Prior to joining Centric Infrastructure, Mr. Palmenberg was Managing Partner and Founder of The Palmenberg Group, LLC, a privately held, boutique consultancy and venture capital firm which specializes in product/service innovation, commercialization, and marketing/sales strategies. Prior to creating his own firm, Mr. Palmenberg was a Partner at Strategy& | PwC (formerly Booz & Company and Booz Allen Hamilton). He began his career with Siemens Westinghouse as an engineer.

Mr. Palmenberg holds an MBA from The Wharton Business School – University of Pennsylvania, a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering Technology from Texas A&M University and is a certified lean manufacturing/six sigma master black belt.
