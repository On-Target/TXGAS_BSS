Loan Tran

Engineer

Ms. Tran serves as an Engineer for Centric Infrastructure Group. During her time at Centric, she has worked closely with the leadership and engineering team on the design and layout of natural gas and fiber optic systems for multiple projects across Texas.

In addition to design, Loan is responsible for interpreting engineering plans and plats and serves as a bridge between various Texas governmental agencies and levels regarding permit approvals. Her partnership with the operations team provides an ease of constructability and ensures a scheduled delivery on time.

Before joining Centric Infrastructure, Loan worked as the Subsurface Utility Engineering Project Manager for Cobb Fendley, a national civil engineering firm. Prior to that, she was a Land and Site Development Project Engineer for a civil engineering firm in the Houston area. She brings in over a total of 8 years of engineering experience.

Ms. Tran holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from University of Houston. Outside of work, Loan is the Vice President member of the Board for Harris County MUD No. 534 and Executive Board member for STEM BRIDGES Houston. She enjoys volunteering, traveling, hiking, playing tennis, cooking, and spending time with her friends and family.
